# Project Overview

This project demonstrates the implementation of an automated email marketing campaign for *TITAN GYM*, a local fitness center. The campaign is designed to engage new subscribers, promote membership offers, provide valuable fitness tips, and collect customer feedback using an automated email workflow.

---

# Project Objectives

- Create an automated email marketing workflow.
- Welcome new subscribers with a personalized email.
- Increase customer engagement through fitness content.
- Promote membership offers.
- Collect customer feedback.
- Improve customer retention and conversions.

# Email Campaigns

### 1. Welcome Email
- Introduces TITAN GYM
- Greets new subscribers
- Encourages them to begin their fitness journey

### 2. Fitness Tips Email
- Shares beginner-friendly fitness advice
- Encourages healthy habits
- Builds trust with subscribers

### 3. Promotional Offer Email
- Announces a limited-time 20% membership discount
- Creates urgency
- Encourages immediate registration

### 4. Success Stories Email
- Highlights member achievements
- Builds credibility
- Motivates potential customers

### 5. Feedback Email
- Requests customer feedback
- Improves customer satisfaction
- Strengthens long-term relationships
